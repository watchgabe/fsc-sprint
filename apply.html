01
Your Brand Blueprint You do it

02
90-Minute Onboarding Call Together
Next, you’ll be onboarded where we review: avatar description, positioning doc, and competitor research. You walk out with strategic clarity on who you’re for, what you stand for, and where you sit in the market.
03
Install Your Frictionless OS Together
The AI-powered content creation system that actually knows who you are, can quickly capture your thoughts, generate endless ideas, and automatically store them in a dedicated dashboard. Everything powered by a growing content brain that lives directly on your desktop and understands everything about you.
04
Build Your Custom Hook Engine My team does it
We’ll build you a dedicated hook engine based on proven content from your competitors and your industry. Drop in a simple idea, rambling, or full script — get back a powerful & proven hook that we can use and test.
05
An Entire Month of Scripts My team does it
We’ll analyze what’s working best for the competitors closest to you. Using proven styles, topics, and techniques, you’ll walk out with 30 ready-to-shoot scripts & storyboards — not 30 ideas, 30 videos ready for production.
06
Your Visual Identity My team does it
A custom visual identity with your fonts, colors, color grades and plug & play templates built directly in Canva for quick and easy access.
07
Content Coaching Together
You’ll learn how to shoot professional content with whatever gear you have — smartphone or actual camera, doesn’t matter. The full rundown: cinematic shooting, B-roll capture, storage, upload, the whole workflow. Along with a proven system to stay consistent and access to content editing services to act as a full stop.
08
Community Access Together
Access to creatives on the same path as you, on the same level as you, and some even ahead of you. Weekly calls, Q&A’s, and trainings to keep you up to speed on all of the trends.
