Phase 01 · Brand Foundation Weeks 1–3
The AI brand brain and your visual identity.
  • Brand Blueprint ICP, content pillars, competitor read, positioning, and voice. All documented and loaded into the AI tools that power your content.
  • Visual Identity Full brand kit. Colors, typography, moodboard, visual system. Built in Canva so you can use it from Day 1.
  • Shot System Blueprint A repeatable visual recipe. Framing, lighting, environment. Your content looks like you every time.
  • Avatar & Market Research Your dream client mapped in detail. A read on where the gaps in your market are.
Phase 02 · Content System Weeks 3–5
The AI-powered engine. Idea to posted, on repeat.
  • Content Dashboard Your pillars, calendar, and batching workflow in one place.
  • Idea-to-Content Engine Voice notes and half-ideas in. Polished content in your voice out.
  • Idea Capture Voice memo to idea bank to ready-to-shoot. Nothing gets lost.
  • AI Prompt Library Pre-loaded with your Brand Blueprint. Hooks, captions, scripts, repurposing.
  • Loom Walkthrough Full system tour so you know how every piece works.
Phase 03 · Funnel Weeks 5–7
Followers into leads. Leads into clients.
  • Lead Magnet A real resource your dream client wants. Designed and written.
  • ManyChat Automation Comment triggers and DM flows. Tested and live.
  • Email Platform Full setup. Your list captures leads from Day 1.
  • Welcome Sequence A 7-email automation that introduces you, builds trust, and warms leads up to your offer.
  • Profile & Bio Bio rewritten, link-in-bio audited, first impression dialed.
Phase 04 · Launch Weeks 7–8
Switch flipped. 30 days of content ready to ship.
  • Full System Launch Brand kit, content system, funnel, automations. All live.
  • 30 Pre-Approved Ideas A month of content mapped: angles, hooks, format. You film and ship.
  • 3 Anchor Posts Scripted Your story, your positioning, your authority piece. Ready to film.
  • Content Training Filming, audio, editing, batching. 1-on-1, recorded for reference.
  • Day 60 Check-In We assess what's landing, what isn't, and where to go next.
  • Full Handoff Every asset, prompt, template, doc, and workflow. Yours forever.
